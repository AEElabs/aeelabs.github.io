Our Services

Comprehensive Engineering Services

From concept schematics to Gerber files, we provide complete PCB design and embedded hardware solutions.

Professional Schematic Design

Comprehensive schematic capture with proper component selection, netlist generation, and design rule verification.

2 Layer PCB Design

Cost-effective double-layer PCB layouts optimized for manufacturing with proper grounding and power distribution.

4 Layer PCB Design

Multi-layer designs with dedicated power and ground planes for improved signal integrity and EMI performance.

6 Layer PCB Design

High-density interconnect designs for complex applications requiring multiple signal routing layers.

High Speed PCB Design

Controlled impedance routing, differential pairs, and length matching for high-speed digital interfaces.

Signal Integrity Design

Analysis and mitigation of signal degradation issues including crosstalk, reflections, and jitter.

Power Integrity Analysis

PDN analysis ensuring stable power delivery with proper decoupling and low-impedance power distribution.

Power Electronics Design

Complete power converter design from specifications to validated prototypes with industry-best efficiency.

SMPS Design

Switched-mode power supply design with optimized magnetics, feedback compensation, and thermal management.

Synchronous Buck Converter

High-efficiency step-down converters with synchronous rectification for minimal power loss and compact form factors.

Boost Converter Design

Step-up DC-DC converters for battery-powered and industrial applications requiring higher output voltages.

Buck-Boost Converter

Versatile converters handling input voltages both above and below the output with seamless mode transitions.

Flyback Converter

Isolated power supply solutions for low to medium power applications with multiple output rails.

Forward Converter

Isolated DC-DC converters with excellent efficiency for medium to high power industrial applications.

LLC Resonant Converter

High-efficiency resonant topology designs achieving zero-voltage switching for minimal switching losses.

High Current LDO Design

Low-dropout linear regulators capable of delivering high current with ultra-low noise for sensitive analog circuits.

Battery Charger Design

Advanced battery management with CC/CV charging profiles for Li-Ion, Li-Po, and lead-acid batteries.

Motor Driver Design

Robust motor control solutions including H-bridge, three-phase inverter, and stepper motor drivers.

Embedded Hardware Design

Our Process

Engineering Design Process

A proven methodology ensuring quality and reliability at every stage of development.

Step 01

Requirement Analysis

Detailed discussion of specifications, constraints, and project goals to establish a clear engineering roadmap.

Step 02

Concept Design

Architecture definition, block diagram creation, and key component selection based on performance requirements.

Step 03

Schematic Design

Detailed schematic capture with proper net labeling, design rule checks, and comprehensive annotation.

Step 04

Simulation

Circuit simulation using LTspice/MATLAB to verify performance, stability, and transient response before layout.

Step 05

PCB Layout

Optimized component placement and routing with attention to signal integrity, thermal management, and EMI/EMC.

Step 06

Design Review

Thorough review checklist covering electrical, mechanical, and manufacturing aspects before release.

Step 07

Gerber Generation

Manufacturing-ready output files with complete fabrication and assembly data packages.

Step 08

Prototype

Quick-turn PCB fabrication and assembly for initial bring-up, debugging, and performance validation.

Step 09

Testing

Comprehensive functional testing, environmental stress testing, and compliance verification.

Step 10

Production Support

Ongoing support during production ramp-up including yield optimization and field feedback integration.
