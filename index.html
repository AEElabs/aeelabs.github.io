About AEE Labs

Engineering Excellence in Every Layer

AEE Labs is a premier electronic hardware design consultancy founded by Abboju Nikhil, a Professional Hardware Design Engineer with an M.Tech in Power Electronics and ex-DRDO apprentice experience. We provide complete electronic hardware development services from concept to production.

Our commitment to professional engineering, high-quality PCB layouts, reliable schematics, and manufacturing support ensures your product transitions smoothly from an idea to a market-ready device. With international support and fast turnaround, we serve clients across the globe.
